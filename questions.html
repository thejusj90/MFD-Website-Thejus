Working together

I help you make clear, well-reasoned decisions about saving, investing, and planning toward goals — starting with a conversation about where you stand today, then a plan you actually understand and can stick to. I don't manage your money directly; you stay in control of every account and transaction.

Mostly young professionals in their 20s and 30s who are earning well but haven't yet built a clear system for saving, investing, and planning — whether that's starting your first SIP or getting organised before a big goal like a house or marriage.

We talk through your current situation, what you're trying to achieve, and where the gaps are. No pitch, no pressure to sign up for anything — it's genuinely meant to give you clarity, whether or not we work together afterward.

Fees & how I'm paid

I'm registered with AMFI as a Mutual Fund Distributor (ARN 363352). That means I earn a distribution commission from the mutual fund house when you invest through me — the same expense ratio you'd pay investing directly, at no extra cost to you. There's no separate advisory fee.

No — I operate as a Mutual Fund Distributor, not an Investment Adviser. That's an important distinction: I can help you select and invest in mutual funds and explain how they work, but I don't provide the kind of personalised, fee-based investment advice a Registered Investment Adviser (RIA) is licensed to give. If you need comprehensive financial planning across all asset classes, an RIA may be a better fit.

Investing basics
