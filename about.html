I've spent the better part of 15 years building and running innovation ecosystems in India — incubators, accelerators, and startup programs that helped founders go from idea to funded company.

Along the way, I noticed the same gap show up again and again outside of work too: smart, capable people — the same ones building great companies — often had no clear system for their own money. Not because they weren't capable of it, but because nobody had ever laid it out simply.

That's what this site and my conversations are for. I'm a registered Mutual Fund Distributor (AMFI ARN 363352), and my job is to give you clarity — not sell you a product. No stock tips, no fund bias, just principles that hold up and a plan you actually understand.
